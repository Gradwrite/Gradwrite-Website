Gradwrite Spring 2026 Wrap-Up

This past weekend we hosted our Gradwrite Spring 2026 tournaments for 36 teams across our 4 sites in Vancouver, Toronto, Ottawa, and online. We want to thank every team that participated, and congratulate the winners of each site: McMath, UST A, Glebe, and Renert, who all went undefeated at their sites. Our feedback form is always open in case you have any questions or feedback for us!

Due to unforeseen circumstances, our Toronto tournament will be split into 2 tournaments, one in-person at Upper Canada College on Saturday, April 25th, and one online on Sunday, April 26th.
