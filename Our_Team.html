We are a group of recent high school graduates passionate about competitive trivia and the trivia community. We love writing, editing, and playing trivia questions! Since our founding in 2023, we've written and run six high-quality trivia tournaments, attracting teams from across Canada and raising over $10000 for education-based charities.

All of us played on the Canadian trivia circuit in high school, and we're eager to give back to the community we spent our high school years with. We aim to provide high-quality, accessible, and affordable trivia events to the trivia circuit and to foster diversity and inclusivity in the trivia sphere.

We hope to see you at our next event!
